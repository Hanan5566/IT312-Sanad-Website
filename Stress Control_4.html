Summary & Key Takeaways

📌 Understanding Stress

Stress is a natural response, but chronic academic stress affects 70% of students. Recognizing it early is the first step to control.

⚡ Identifying Triggers

Academic, personal, and social factors all contribute to stress. Knowing your specific triggers helps in choosing the right relief method.

🧠 Core Techniques

Deep breathing and journaling are highly effective for immediate relief, while time blocking prevents stress before it starts.

🎯 Long-term Success

Mental health is directly linked to academic performance. Consistency in self-care leads to better grades and a healthier mindset.

📋 Daily Checklist for a Stress-Free Life

  • ✅ Practice 5 minutes of deep breathing every morning.
  • ✅ Use time-blocking to manage your daily assignments.
  • ✅ Take regular short walks to clear your mind.
  • ✅ Maintain a healthy sleep schedule (7-9 hours).
  • ✅ Replace negative self-talk with positive affirmations.
