Introduction to Stress Control

Topic Overview

Studies show that over 70% of students experience academic stress regularly. Learning to control stress not only improves your mental health but also boosts your academic performance significantly.

Causes of Academic Stress

Academic Factors

  • Exam Anxiety
  • Fear of Failure
  • Overwhelming Assignments

Personal Factors

  • Lack of Sleep
  • Negative Self-Talk

Social Factors

  • Peer Pressure
  • Isolation
