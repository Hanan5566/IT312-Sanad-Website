Summary & Key Takeaways

📌 Introduction

80% of success depends on study method, not hours. Focus on techniques, not time.

⚡ Best Study Methods

Feynman Technique (95% retention) and Active Recall (85%) are most effective.

🧠 Stress Management

Meditation (10 min) and Deep Breathing (5 min) reduce stress significantly.

📋 Quick Checklist

  • ✅ Use active recall for memorization subjects
  • ✅ Apply Feynman technique for concepts
  • ✅ Take 5 min deep breathing breaks
