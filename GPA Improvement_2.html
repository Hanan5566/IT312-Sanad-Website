GPA Calculation & Tracking

🧮 Quick GPA Formula

GPA = (Total Grade Points) ÷ (Total Credit Hours)

For a course with 3 credit hours, earning an A grade (4.0) results in 12 grade points. Example: Grade Points = 4.0 × 3 = 12
